NOSTALGIC
  1. Total Overdose
    I like this game as my faviorte nostalgic game is becase its the first ever pc game that I own as a child. This game has a very funny and wacky story. We are Ramiro Cruz a Mexican gunslinger investigating our fathers murder. There is a weird power system in this game called loco moves these help us by giving us extra ability such as immunity to gun etc. All in all this was a pretty good game for its standard.
  2. Halo: Combat Evolved
    This is my personal from the halo series. It has a great story line and intresting enemies to say the least but. This is a more futurestic game. we crash land on a ring shaped planet and our objective is to survive and extract from the planet while learning about the advance life form and the planet itself.
  3. Duck Hunt
    Duck hunt originally released for the snes(Super Nintendo Entertainment System). I personally got it by modding my ps1 that allowed cross play. The premise is simple we are a dog with a gun hunting ducks and the simplicity is what made me love it.
